About Mr. Detail MN

At Mr. Detail, we specialize in mobile auto detailing services designed to bring professional car care directly to your door anywhere in the Minneapolis area. Founded on a passion for excellence and convenience, our operation is 100% mobile, allowing you to experience top-tier paint correction, ceramic coating, and complete car detailing without ever needing to visit a shop.

Our Mission

We are committed to restoring and protecting your vehicle’s beauty with comprehensive care tailored to your schedule and lifestyle. Using industry-leading products and techniques, we deliver showroom-quality results that enhance your car's appearance and safeguard its value.

Why Choose Us?

Mr. Detail MN prides itself on flexibility, professionalism, and quality. Our mobile setup means you save time, avoiding the hassle of dropping off your vehicle. Our expert technicians are passionate about detail and take great care with every vehicle entrusted to us.

Whether you want routine maintenance or a major transformation, Mr. Detail delivers superior mobile auto care with personalized service and outstanding attention to detail.
