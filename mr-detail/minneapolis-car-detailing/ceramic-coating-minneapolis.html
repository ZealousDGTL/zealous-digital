Give your vehicle the ultimate defense against the harsh Minneapolis elements with Mr. Detail’s professional ceramic coating service. Our premium ceramic coating not only enhances your car’s paint depth and gloss but also forms a durable shield that protects against damage from salt, UV rays, road debris, and contaminants common to Minnesota’s climate.

Ceramic coating is a specially formulated liquid polymer that chemically bonds to your vehicle’s paint, creating a semi-permanent barrier. This layer repels water, dirt, and grime, making your car easier to clean while preserving that fresh-from-the-showroom shine for years to come.

Our experts meticulously prepare your vehicle’s surface with thorough washing and paint correction before applying multiple layers of ceramic coating. This careful process ensures the maximum durability and flawless finish you expect from a professional-grade service.
