What maintenance is required to keep a power washer operating optimally?

Routine maintenance includes cleaning the nozzles, checking hose connections, inspecting oil levels, and flushing the system after use. Regular servicing by professionals extends equipment life and prevents costly downtime, so we recommend scheduled checkups tailored to your usage pattern.

What detergents and soap products do you recommend for use with power washers?

We offer a variety of industrial-grade detergents designed for compatibility with our power washers. These include biodegradable, low-alkaline, and heavy-duty formulas to tackle grease, grime, and other contaminants safely without damaging surfaces or equipment.

How do hot water power washers differ from cold water ones?

Hot water power washers are ideal for removing oils, grease, and stubborn dirt due to the enhanced cleaning power of heated water. Cold water units are typically used for general surface cleaning where heat is unnecessary. We help you decide based on your cleaning environment and target soils.

What safety precautions should I follow when operating a power washer?

Operators should use protective gear such as gloves, goggles, and non-slip footwear. Maintain a safe distance from power washing surfaces, avoid electrical hazards, and follow all manufacturer safety instructions. Proper training is recommended to ensure user safety and machine longevity.

Do you offer training on how to properly use and maintain power washers?

Yes, we provide onsite and remote training sessions tailored to your team’s experience level. Training covers operational best practices, safety protocols, routine maintenance, and troubleshooting to help you maximize performance and equipment lifespan.

How should I store my power washer when not in use?

Store your power washer in a dry, sheltered area away from freezing temperatures to prevent damage. Drain and flush the system after use, and protect hoses and connections. We offer guidance on seasonal storage to extend equipment life.
